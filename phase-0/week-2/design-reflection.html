What are the main things someone should know about design and user experience?

From my personal experience, it needs to be intuitive, and people should be able to go on there and accomplish whatever it is with out needing to invest too much time in understanding how everything works. It also needs to look good on the eyes, not take too long to load, and it needs to be responsive.

What is user experience design and why is it valuable?

User experience will make or break your app. If people in general are not happy after leaving your site, then you are not going to be successful in this business. The hardest part to all this is making something that anyone can use, not just computer guys who have thousands of hours invested

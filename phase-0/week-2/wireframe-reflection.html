What is a wireframe?

A wireframe is a basic bare bones blueprint or map of what you plan on creating.

What are the benefits of wireframing?

Gives a foundation and can be used as a general guide when you first start building. It is necessary to make a plan before you start creating any code, this will lessen the chance of creating something that won't work, and/or something that leads to a dead end. Coding without any pre planning is like driving blindfolded, you need to have a well thought out map to guide you and keep you thinking ahead. That's what wireframing can do.
